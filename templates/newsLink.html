Reliable News Sources

United Kingdom

BBC News
The British Broadcasting Corporation (BBC) provides impartial coverage and has a wide international audience.
The Guardian
The Guardian is a UK-based news outlet known for its investigative journalism and in-depth reporting.

United States

CNN
Cable News Network (CNN) is a major US news network known for its 24-hour news coverage.
Reuters
Reuters is an international news organization that provides breaking news and financial information globally.
Associated Press (AP)
AP is a US-based news agency known for delivering fast, accurate news from around the world.
The New York Times
The New York Times is an American newspaper with a significant influence and a focus on thorough journalism.
NPR
National Public Radio (NPR) offers in-depth reporting and analysis of news and cultural issues in the US.
The Washington Post
The Washington Post is a major American newspaper with a focus on national politics and policy.

Singapore

The Straits Times
The Straits Times is the most widely read newspaper in Singapore, known for its comprehensive coverage of local and international news.
Channel NewsAsia (CNA)
Channel NewsAsia (CNA) is a Singapore-based news network that provides news and analysis on global developments with an Asian perspective.
